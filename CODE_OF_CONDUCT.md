# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that:

- Everyone is treated with respect and dignity
- Diversity of experience, perspective, and background is valued
- No harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior is tolerated
- Conflicts are resolved constructively and professionally

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- **Being respectful** of different opinions, experiences, and backgrounds
- **Being constructive** in feedback and code reviews
- **Being inclusive** and welcoming to newcomers
- **Being collaborative** and supportive of others
- **Being patient** with questions and explaining concepts clearly
- **Acknowledging mistakes** and learning from them

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or insulting comments
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, personal attacks, or inflammatory remarks
- Doxing or sharing private information without consent
- Exclusionary language or gatekeeping
- Any form of discrimination based on identity

## Enforcement

Community leaders will:

1. **Address violations** promptly and fairly
2. **Seek context** before making decisions
3. **Respect privacy** of those reporting and accused
4. **Take proportional action** (warning, temporary ban, permanent ban)

To report violations contact maintainers privately.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Code and documentation
- Discussions and comments
- Project-related communications
- Official events and meetups
